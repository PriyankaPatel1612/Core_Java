  Unary Operators
  Binary Operators
  Conditional OPerators

#Unary Operators
  Increment(++)
  decrement(--)
  preincrement(++a)
  postincrement(a++)
  predecrement(--a)
  postdecrement(a--)

  Arithmatic Operator(+,-,/,*)
